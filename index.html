Into The Woods Festival

12-18 July, Amersfoort
For those who are delving for something out of the mainstream, the Into the Woods Festival is pushing the boundaries of house and techno. Now over a decade old, they mix nature, surrealism and a lineup of underground and up and coming artists to bring you to a magic fairy-tale world. As one of the best known in the country, every year Into The Woods gives us something we weren't expecting.
